The Maison Protocol

A guide to your bespoke acquisition experience.

I. The Archive

Navigate our curated collections via the main menu. Tap any product image to experience our cinematic zoom and view the masterpiece in absolute detail.

II. The Wishlist ( ♥ )

Tap the heart icon on any piece to save it to your private collection. You may access your curated selections anytime via the floating heart icon on your screen.

III. Securing Pieces ( 🛒 Cart )

Select your exact size and tap 'Cart' to reserve an item. When you are ready to finalize, open your floating Cart box to review your acquisitions.

IV. Absolute Payment & Bank Transfer

Within your Cart, proceed to Checkout. You may finalize your acquisition instantly via our automated Paystack gateway, or select the Bank Transfer option to view our official Dollars account details for a direct wire.

V. Direct Concierge ( 💬 Buy )

For immediate VIP assistance, tap the 'Buy' button. This will connect you directly to our private WhatsApp concierge to arrange bespoke delivery and personal styling.
